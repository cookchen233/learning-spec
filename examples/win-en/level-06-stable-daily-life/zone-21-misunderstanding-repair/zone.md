## Zone Identity

- misunderstanding-repair zone

## Reward Meaning

- 我拿下了误听、误解、口音、噪音、电话、信息错误、卡住后修复并继续沟通的地盘。

## Included Capabilities

- capability-01-recover-from-not-hearing-or-not-understanding
- capability-02-repair-wrong-assumption-or-wrong-information
- capability-03-handle-accent-noise-speed-and-phone-call
- capability-04-ask-for-confirmation-in-writing
- capability-05-restate-apologize-and-continue-after-breakdown
- capability-06-protect-key-information-before-ending

## Completion Signal

- 我可以在没听清、听错、理解错、对方语速快、有口音、环境嘈杂或电话中继续修复沟通。
- 我可以请求对方写下来、发短信 / 邮件确认、复述关键信息并纠正错误理解。
- 我可以在沟通崩掉后道歉、重述问题、回到任务主线。

## Out of Scope

- 不处理复杂争辩、法律责任澄清或高压谈判。
